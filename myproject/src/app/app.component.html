To read ebooks, you don’t need a tablet or e-reader. You can read books online, in your internet browser. Nowadays, the internet is a major tool to

find products we need. With more and more content going digital. But what “read books online” means exactly? It means you won’t need any of these:

* a special device, like an Kindle e-reader or Samsung tablet,

* a special application or browser extension.

All you need is a computer connected to the internet, and the application you use most: the internet browser like Chrome, Firefox or Internet Explorer.
